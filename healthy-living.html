Mindfulness for Beginners

May 5, 2025 By Wellness Coach Sarah

Mindfulness can reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance emotional regulation. Here's how to get started with simple practices:

1. The 5-Minute Breathing Exercise

  1. Find a quiet space and sit comfortably
  2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath
  3. Notice the sensation of air entering and leaving your nostrils
  4. When your mind wanders (it will!), gently bring focus back to your breath
  5. Set a timer for 5 minutes

2. Mindful Eating

Try this with one meal this week:

  • Before eating, observe your food's colors and smells
  • Take small bites and chew slowly
  • Notice textures and flavors
  • Put your fork down between bites

Sleep Hygiene: 10 Tips for Better Rest

May 3, 2025 By Sleep Specialist Dr. Lee

Quality sleep is foundational to health. Implement these evidence-based strategies to improve your sleep:

  1. Consistent Schedule: Go to bed and wake up at the same time daily
  2. Blue Light Reduction: Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  3. Bedroom Environment: Keep room cool (60-67Â°F), dark, and quiet
  4. Caffeine Cutoff: No caffeine after 2pm
  5. Wind Down Routine: Develop a 30-minute pre-sleep ritual
  6. Daylight Exposure: Get morning sunlight to regulate circadian rhythm
  7. Limit Naps: If you nap, keep it under 30 minutes
  8. Exercise: Regular activity improves sleep (but not right before bed)
  9. Mind Your Meals: Avoid heavy meals before bedtime
  10. Stress Management: Practice relaxation techniques
