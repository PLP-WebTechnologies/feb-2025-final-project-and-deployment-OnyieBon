Meal Prep Like a Pro

June 8, 2023 By Nutritionist Tom

Meal prepping can save you time, money, and help you stick to your health goals. Here's our step-by-step guide:

  1. Plan your meals for the week
  2. Make a detailed shopping list
  3. Dedicate 2-3 hours on your prep day
  4. Cook proteins in bulk
  5. Pre-cut vegetables
  6. Portion everything into containers

Pro tip: Invest in good quality glass containers that can go from fridge to microwave.
