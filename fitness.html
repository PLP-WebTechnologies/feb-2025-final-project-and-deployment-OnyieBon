Home Workout Routine (No Equipment)

June 12, 2023 By Trainer Jessica

You don't need a gym membership to get in great shape. This 30-minute routine can be done anywhere and targets all major muscle groups.

The Routine

Complete 3 rounds of the following circuit with 60 seconds rest between rounds:

Exercise Duration/Reps
Jumping Jacks 1 minute
Bodyweight Squats 15 reps
Push-ups 10-15 reps
Plank 30-60 seconds
Lunges 10 each leg

5K Training Plan for Beginners

June 5, 2023 By Runner Mike

Ready to run your first 5K? This 8-week plan will gradually build your endurance without overwhelming you.

Week 1-2: Building a Base

  • Monday: 20 min walk/jog (1 min jog/2 min walk)
  • Wednesday: 20 min walk/jog
  • Friday: 25 min walk/jog
  • Saturday: 30 min walk

Week 3-4: Increasing Endurance

  • Monday: 25 min jog/walk (2 min jog/1 min walk)
  • Wednesday: 25 min jog/walk
  • Friday: 30 min jog/walk
  • Saturday: 35 min walk
